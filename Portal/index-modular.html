📸 Snapshots

Snapshots are immutable records of your conversation history. Each snapshot captures the complete context at a specific moment in time. They're automatically created based on conversation length and can be manually triggered via the Checkpoint button.

💾 Checkpoints

Create a checkpoint to manually save the current conversation state. Click the "💾 Checkpoint" button in the header to capture the current context. Checkpoints help preserve important conversation milestones and enable you to review key decision points later.

🧠 Memory System

The AI maintains context through an immutable volume. Each interaction is recorded as a snapshot, creating a complete audit trail. The system uses a Gold Master approach—snapshots are cryptographically verified and cannot be altered, ensuring conversation integrity.

🎨 Image & Video Generation

Generate images and videos by simply describing what you want. Use natural language like "Generate an image of a sunset over mountains" or "Create a video of ocean waves." The AI uses Google's Imagen models to bring your descriptions to life.

🔊 Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Convert AI responses to natural-sounding speech using Gemini Pro's TTS preview engine. Click the 🔊 button to hear responses read aloud. The system supports multiple voices and natural prosody for realistic audio output.

🎙️ SSML Script Generation

Create advanced audio scripts using Speech Synthesis Markup Language (SSML). Control emphasis, pauses, pitch, and rate. Example: "Generate a script with <break time='2s'/> for dramatic pauses" or "Use <emphasis level='strong'> for key points."
